Core Contributor

Simplified Coding

Computer Science Tutor

Cap-O-Meter

Gymnasium of Epanomi

November 2022—June 2023

Our Educational Robotics & STEM club in the Gymnasium of Epanomi developed Cap-O-Meter. This device allows schools and other institutions to count how many plastic bottle caps they have collected in order to get wheelchairs for students in need.
